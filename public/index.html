About Me

The artist behind the lens

Om Kumar - Developer & Photographer

I'm a MERN stack web developer with a passionate hobby for photography. While I build digital experiences by day, I capture visual stories by any chance I get. My journey in photography began as a way to document life's beautiful moments, and it has grown into a creative outlet that complements my technical work.

All my photographs are captured and edited on my iPhone XR using the Lightroom CC mobile application. I believe that great photography isn't about the gear but about the vision and perspective. My work focuses on finding beauty in everyday scenes and transforming them into compelling visual narratives.

You can follow my photographic journey on Instagram @captured.by.om where I regularly share my latest captures and edits.

My Photography Setup:

  • Primary Device: iPhone XR
  • Editing Software: Lightroom CC Mobile
  • Specialties: Street Photography, Urban Landscapes, Portraits
  • Approach: Minimalist compositions, Bold colors, Emotional storytelling
